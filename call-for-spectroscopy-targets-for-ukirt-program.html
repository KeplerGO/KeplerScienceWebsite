Call for Spectroscopy Targets for UKIRT Program

Mon 17 August 2015   Knicole Colon

The Kepler/K2 project office has been awarded 30 hours to use UIST on UKIRT for spectroscopic characterization of K2 targets. The aim of the UKIRT program is to provide a uniform catalog of K-band spectra of K2 targets of interest, with a focus on cool exoplanet hosts discovered by K2. We expect to acquire spectra of ~90 K2 targets in the 2015B semester. Targets from Campaigns 0, 3, and 4 are visible around December/January, August/September, and November, respectively. Members of the community are encouraged to submit targets for this program to the GO Office, keeping in mind that all spectra will be made publicly available on the ExoFOP.
