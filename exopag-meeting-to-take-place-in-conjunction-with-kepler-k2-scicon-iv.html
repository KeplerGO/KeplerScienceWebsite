ExoPAG meeting to take place in conjunction with Kepler & K2 SciCon IV

News article posted on 09 May 2017 by Geert Barentsen

NASA's Exoplanet Exploration Program Analysis Group (ExoPAG) will hold its sixteenth meeting on Sunday, June 18, 2017 in Mountain View, CA, one day before the start of Kepler & K2 SciCon IV in the same city.

ExoPAG is responsible for soliciting and coordinating community input into the development and execution of NASA’s Exoplanet Exploration Program (ExEP).

ExoPAG meetings are open to the entire scientific community, and offer an opportunity to participate in discussions of scientific and technical issues in exoplanet exploration. The schedule for the meeting on June 18th may be found on the ExoPAG meeting webpage.
