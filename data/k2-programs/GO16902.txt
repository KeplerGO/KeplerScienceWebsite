# Summary of K2 Program GO16902

Title: Exoplanets Revisited, a proposal to re-observe confirmed exoplanet systems in K2 C16/C18

PI: Omohundro, Mark (Citizen Scientist / PlanetHunters.org)
CoIs: 

It is proposed that the confirmed exoplanet systems that cross over into the fields of view of the K2 C16 and C18 campaigns be observed in long cadence by the Kepler spacecraft. Twelve (12) targets were identified using the K2 Office's k2fov package, and nine (9) of these targets were previously observed in C5 as well.
