K2 Campaign 14 raw data available

News article posted on 25 Aug 2017 by Geert Barentsen

The raw cadence data for K2 Campaign 14 were successfully downlinked from the spacecraft in the weekend of August 19th and are now available for download from the K2 archive at MAST.

The use of the raw, uncalibrated data files requires an intimate understanding of their format and caveats. For nearly all scientific investigations, we recommend that users wait for the calibrated products to become available. Pipeline processing has started and the calibrated data products are expected to become available in the course of October 2017.
