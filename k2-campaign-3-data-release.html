K2 Campaign 3 Data Release

Date Thu 09 July 2015 By Geert Barentsen

Target Pixels Files and Light Curves from K2 Campaign 3 are now in the process of being transferred to MAST, and work is ongoing to prepare the accompanying data release notes. We hope that the data will become available to the public by the end of next week.

The Campaign 3 target list included 16,375 standard long cadence and 55 standard short cadence targets. In addition we have included large pixel masks over dwarf planet (225088) 2007 OR10, the planet Neptune and its moon Nereid.

Keep an eye on this blog for the release announcement.
