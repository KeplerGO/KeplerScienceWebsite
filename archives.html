News for scientists

This page provides news updates to help the scientific community propose targets for K2 and analyze Kepler and/or K2 data. These updates will be transmitted by email exploder at non-regular intervals. If you would like to receive this newsletter/blog then sign up by sending an email to keplergonews-request@lists.arc.nasa.gov with the subject heading subscribe. If you would like to stop receiving the newsletter then send email to the same address with the subject heading unsubscribe. You can also subscribe to the RSS feed to receive the latest news.

For general news about Kepler's results and operations, please visit NASA's Kepler and K2 mission website and its Mission Manager Blog instead.
