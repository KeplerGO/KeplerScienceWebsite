Campaign 20 proposal information

Target proposals for K2 Campaign 20 continue to be requested by Thursday, August 16, 2018.

Key facts:

  • Proposals consist of up to 2 pages for small programs (< 1000 targets) and up to 4 pages for large programs (1000 targets or more), plus a target table.
  • Proposers are encouraged to use the proposal template available in Word format and in Latex format.
  • The proposal and accompanying target table must be must be submitted via email to keplergo@mail.arc.nasa.gov.
  • Although Campaign 20 is at risk of not being executed due to Kepler's low fuel status, the project does not currently have information that precludes Campaign 20 from taking place.

All proposals must follow the instructions detailed in ROSES D.7 (K2 GO Cycle 7) and clarified on our proposal preparation page. Please study these materials carefully.

Questions? Contact keplergo@mail.arc.nasa.gov or phone us at +1-650-604-2784.
