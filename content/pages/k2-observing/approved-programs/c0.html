Campaign 0 was a full-length engineering test and is notably shorter in duration than later campaigns. This in the only campaign where the target list was not selected by peer-review. The Campaign 0 target list includes the open clusters M35 (NGC 2168) and NGC 2158. These were observed using 154 separate 50x50 pixel masks.
